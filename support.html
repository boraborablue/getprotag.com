Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an episode?

Write at least 3 journal entries, then tap "Ready to Produce!" on the Dailies tab. Select your entries and choose a genre to generate your episode.

Is my journal private?

Yes! Your journal entries are encrypted and stored securely. Real names are automatically replaced with fictional names before any AI processing. We never use your data to train AI models.

How do I protect names in my episodes?

Go to Production Notes and add the people and places you write about. Assign fictional names, and Protag will automatically swap them in your episodes.

Can I change my episode after it's generated?

Yes! After generation, you'll see a preview where you can provide feedback and request adjustments before finalizing your episode.

What genres are available?

Currently: Rom-Com, Workplace Sitcom, Drama, Reality TV, and Mystery. More genres coming soon!

Why is my episode different from what I wrote?

Protag transforms your real experiences into entertaining TV-style stories. The AI adds dramatic structure, dialogue, and narrative flair while keeping the essence of your day.
